About HotBite

Fast. Simple. Delivered. Canteen Meals Made Easy.

System Overview

HotBite is a comprehensive digital canteen management system designed to streamline campus dining experiences. Our platform connects students, staff, and canteen operators through an intuitive interface that simplifies ordering and delivery processes. Built with modern web technologies, HotBite ensures a fast and reliable food service experience that adapts to the evolving needs of educational institutions.

Key Features

  • 📱

    Mobile-First Design

    Responsive interface optimized for smartphones and tablets

  • ⚡

    Real-Time Ordering

    Instant order placement with live status updates

  • 🚴

    Volunteer Delivery

    Student and community volunteers help deliver orders efficiently and may earn incentives through participation

  • 🧠

    Intuitive Interface

    Simple and user-friendly layout for both students and staff

  • 📊

    Analytics Dashboard

    Comprehensive reporting tools for canteen management

  • 🔔

    Canteen Status Updates

    Live canteen open/close status for smoother ordering and operation

Target Users & Beneficiaries

HotBite is primarily designed for the Gordon College community. Students benefit from a more convenient ordering process and faster service. Faculty and staff can enjoy streamlined meal access that fits their work schedules.

The primary beneficiary of the project is Ms. Mellisa Lacanlale, owner of Lacanlale Kitchenette, located at #38 Bacon Street, East Tapinac, Olongapo City, near the Gordon College employee entrance.
