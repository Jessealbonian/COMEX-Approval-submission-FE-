Student Volunteer Terms and Conditions

By joining the HotBite Volunteer Delivery Program, you agree to the following terms:

1. Volunteer Conduct and Responsibility

You agree to perform deliveries professionally, follow campus policies, and handle every assigned order with care and accuracy.

2. Availability and Assignment Confirmation

When you switch your status to Available, you indicate that you are ready to receive potential delivery tasks.

A confirmation modal will appear when an order delivery is offered to you. You must manually Accept or Decline every delivery assignment.

Selecting Accept Delivery confirms your commitment to complete the task.

Selecting Decline informs the staff that you are unable to take that specific task.

Failing to respond repeatedly or declining excessively may result in a review of your volunteer status.

3. Delivery Responsibilities

Once you accept an order delivery, you are responsible for completing the delivery within the expected timeframe. Only 20 minutes are allotted after the preferred time set for the order to complete and deliver it. Any sudden issues or delays must be reported immediately to the canteen staff.

4. Prohibited Actions

You agree not to:

  • Mark orders as delivered without completing them
  • Accept order deliveries you cannot fulfill
  • Misuse the availability toggle to avoid or manipulate tasks
  • Delay or tamper with the order assignment process
  • Attempt to accept deliveries while having active personal orders

5. Safety and Proper Conduct

You must carry out deliveries safely and with proper behavior around campus.

6. Data Use and Privacy

Your availability, task confirmations, and delivery performance may be collected for system management and fairness. Your personal data is handled according to the system's Data Privacy Policy.

7. Suspension or Removal

The administrators may suspend or remove volunteer access for repeated misconduct, declines without reason, task avoidance, or violation of these terms.

8. Updates to Terms

These Terms may be updated. By continuing to serve as a volunteer, you agree to any future revisions.
